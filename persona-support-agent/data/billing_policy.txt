Billing Policy

Our SaaS platform offers monthly and annual subscription billing.

Billing is processed automatically based on the active subscription plan.

Supported billing cycles:

* Monthly
* Annual

Accepted payment methods:

* Credit Card
* Debit Card
* UPI
* Bank Transfer

Invoices are generated automatically after successful payment.

Customers can download invoices from the billing dashboard.

Late or failed payments may result in:

* Service suspension
* Restricted access
* Delayed renewals

Repeated payment failures may trigger account review.

Billing disputes must be reported within 7 days of invoice generation.

For billing-related issues, contact billing support with:

* Account ID
* Invoice number
* Transaction reference
