Refund Policy

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of payment.

Refunds are only eligible under the following conditions:

* Duplicate charges
* Failed service activation
* Billing system errors
* Incorrect invoice generation

Refunds are generally not eligible for:

* Partial subscription usage
* Expired billing periods
* Custom enterprise contracts

Refund request process:

1. Contact billing support
2. Submit payment details
3. Provide issue explanation

Required information:

* Invoice number
* Transaction ID
* Payment date

Refund processing time:
5–7 business days

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method.
