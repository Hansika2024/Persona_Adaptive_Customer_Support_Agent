# Payment Failure Guide

## Overview

Payment failures may occur during subscription purchase, renewal, or upgrades.

---

## Common Causes

* Insufficient account balance
* Card declined
* Bank authorization failure
* Expired card
* Payment gateway timeout

---

## Common Errors

### Payment Declined

The payment provider rejected the transaction.

### Gateway Timeout

The payment request exceeded response time limits.

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Verify payment details.
2. Ensure sufficient balance.
3. Retry after a few minutes.
4. Use alternative payment method.

Supported payment methods:

* Credit Card
* Debit Card
* UPI
* Bank Transfer

---

## Important Notes

Repeated failed payments may temporarily block subscription upgrades.

If payment was deducted but service was not activated, contact billing support immediately.
